Ping Pong Game!

Enter any single number to play a fun game of Ping Pong with your browser!.

This web app uses JavaScript to respond to user input with either a list of numbers, "ping," "pong," or "pingpong".

  1. Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"
  2. Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"
  3. Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 are replaced with "pingpong"
